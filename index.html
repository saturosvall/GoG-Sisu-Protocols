SISU Locations:

SISU α

In the heart of the Milky Way, Earth stands as a beacon of life, guarded by a united force. As intergalactic invaders seek to steal Earth's vital photons, the defenders rise to protect their home, harnessing the power of the cosmos to thwart the impending threat.

SISU β

Within the enigmatic Void Veil Galaxy, the serene planet Novaris becomes the battleground for an epic clash. Invaders, drawn to the brilliance of Novaris' photons, face the resistance of the planet's advanced mecha guardians, determined to preserve their cosmic sanctuary.

SISU γ

In the distant R-V3TO Galaxy, the celestial beauty of Planet Stella becomes the target of nefarious forces. As invaders attempt to seize the life-giving photons, Stella's defenders, a coalition of alien species, unite to repel the extraterrestrial menace and safeguard their galactic jewel.

SISU δ

High above the mystical Ilmatarintaivasilma, nestled in the Northern Kingdom, a cosmic ballet unfolds. When malevolent entities threaten to drain the essence of life, the ethereal guardians of Pohjolan Valtakunta rise, weaving magic and celestial power to shield their realm.

SISU ε

On the vibrant Veridian Prime in the Ulgur system, a saga unfolds against those who covet its radiant photons. Ulgur's planetary protectors, equipped with advanced technology, engage in an interstellar battle, determined to secure the future of their flourishing world.

Threats:

Asteroids

DESTROY

Navigate through the cosmic debris field as you destroy relentless asteroids hurdling toward the vulnerable planet. Swiftly eliminate these rocky threats to safeguard the celestial realm.

Baby-Asteroids

DESTROY

Beware of the agile and smaller baby-asteroids accompanying their larger counterparts. They may be diminutive, but their numbers pose a significant threat—obliterate them to ensure the safety of the planet.

Blabbermouths

COLLECT

Gather the elusive Blabbermouths, funny-looking alien species with an insatiable hunger for photons—the planet's most precious resource. Each collected Blabbermouth enhances the planet's defense system.

Space Orks

DESTROY

Face the menacing Space Orks, fierce and relentless invaders seeking to breach the planet's defenses. Employ your skills and firepower to annihilate these intergalactic marauders and protect the vulnerable world.

Spacehornets

COLLECT

Collect the Space Hornets that drift through the cosmos, harvesting their energy. Harness the power of these cosmic beings to fortify against imminent threats.

Astro Mecha Bats

COLLECT

Seek out and collect the Astro Mecha Bats, futuristic mechanical beings that hold the essence of technological prowess. Accumulate their energy to enhance the planet's capabilities.

Mecha Piraija

DESTROY

Confront the Mecha Piranhas — a swarm of mechanical predators threatening the planet's safety. Employ strategic maneuvers and firepower to eliminate these mechanized aquatic threats.

Mecha Younglings

DESTROY

Face the challenge of destroying the Mecha Younglings, emerging robotic entities that may be smaller but are no less dangerous. Eradicate them swiftly to prevent a potential threat escalation.
